### Material Options

#### PLA (Standard Filament)
**Best for:** Decorative items, home d√©cor, gifts

**Properties:**
- Eco-friendly and biodegradable
- Smooth finish
- Wide color range
- Rigid and durable

**Finish Options:** Matte, gloss, natural

**Price:** Standard pricing

---

#### ABS (High-Strength Filament)
**Best for:** Functional items, outdoor use, items needing durability

**Properties:**
- Heat-resistant
- Impact-resistant
- Very durable
- Slightly rougher finish

**Finish Options:** Matte, gloss

**Price:** +20% from standard

---

#### Standard Resin
**Best for:** Highly detailed models, miniatures, collectibles

**Properties:**
- Exceptional detail
- Smooth surface
- Excellent for intricate designs
- Beautiful finish

**Finish Options:** Natural smooth, gloss, matte

**Price:** +30% from standard

---

#### Premium Hand-Painted
**Best for:** Display pieces, gifts, collectibles

**Properties:**
- Professional hand-painting
- Custom color schemes
- Artistic finishing
- One-of-a-kind results

**Finish Options:** Custom painted, metallic finishes

**Price:** +150% from standard (labor-intensive)

## Section 9: FAQs

### Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions**

---

**Q: How long does shipping take?**

A: Most orders ship within 3-5 business days. Standard UK delivery adds another 2-3 days. Rush options available for urgent orders.

---

**Q: Can I request custom colors?**

A: Absolutely! If the standard color options don't suit, contact us and we can print in custom colors for most models (small additional fee may apply).

---

**Q: Are these models exclusive to Quirkable?**

A: Yes! Quirkable Originals are designed in-house or by our partner artists exclusively for our shop. You won't find them elsewhere.

---

**Q: Can I see the print before it ships?**

A: For hand-painted models, we automatically send photos before shipping. For other finishes, just ask and we're happy to send preview photos!

---

**Q: Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?**

A: Yes. If there's a quality issue or you're not happy with your print, contact us within 14 days and we'll make it right with a reprint or full refund.

---

**Q: Can I order in bulk for events or corporate gifts?**

A: Definitely! We offer bulk discounts and can handle large orders. Contact us for a custom quote and we can discuss customization options.

---

**Q: Are the prints durable?**

A: Yes! PLA and ABS prints are quite durable for decorative and light functional use. Resin prints are strong and detailed. We can advise on the best material for your intended use.

---

**Q: Can I request a new design to be added to the collection?**

A: We love suggestions! While we can't promise every request will be added, we actively consider customer ideas for new designs. Submit your idea through our contact form.
