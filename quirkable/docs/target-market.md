# Target Market & Positioning

**Understanding Quirkable's Customers**

---

## Market Overview

Quirkable serves the UK market for 3D printing services, positioning itself as the premium, customer-focused alternative to generic online print shops and the go-to solution for projects that require expertise, care, and creative problem-solving.

---

## Target Market Segments

**Profile:**
- Ages 18-55
- Engaged in creative pursuits: miniature gaming, model building, cosplay, crafts
- Moderate to high disposable income for hobby spending
- Tech-savvy but may lack 3D modeling skills
- Active in online communities (Reddit, Discord, forums)

**Needs:**
- High-quality miniatures and game pieces
- Custom cosplay armor and props
- Unique decorative items
- Replacement parts for collectibles
- Affordable yet professional results

**Quirkable Services They Use:**
- Ready-Made 3D Models (Quirkable Originals)
- Bespoke Printing Service
- File Fixing & Repair

**Pain Points Quirkable Solves:**
- "My printer can't handle this level of detail"
- "I downloaded a file but it won't print"
- "I need this in a specific size/material"
- "I want something unique, not mass-produced"

**Marketing Approach:**

- Tutorial content about file preparation
- Seasonal themed collections

## Market Positioning

### Competitive Landscape

**vs. Generic Online Print Shops:**
- **Quirkable advantage:** File repair, design services, customer support, quality finishing
- **Price position:** Premium but justified by value-added services

**vs. Local Makerspaces:**
- **Quirkable advantage:** Professional quality, no learning curve, full service
- **Price position:** Higher but saves time and guarantees results

**vs. Owning a 3D Printer:**
- **Quirkable advantage:** No maintenance, multiple technologies, expert knowledge
- **Price position:** Pay-per-project vs. upfront equipment cost

**vs. Industrial Prototyping Services:**
- **Quirkable advantage:** More accessible, faster for small projects, better communication
- **Price position:** Lower cost for small-batch and one-off projects

---

## Brand Positioning Statement

**For** UK-based hobbyists, small businesses, and creatives **who need** high-quality 3D printed objects, custom designs, or file repair services, **Quirkable is** the 3D printing service **that** combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving to bring any idea to life. **Unlike** generic print shops, **Quirkable** handles the entire journey from concept to finished product with care, precision, and personality.
