## Section 9: FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### Section Headline
**Questions? We've Got Answers.**

---

### FAQ 1

**Question:** What file formats do you accept?

**Answer:**
We accept STL, OBJ, 3MF, STEP, and most CAD export formats. If you're not sure about your file, just upload it‚Äîwe'll let you know if there are any issues.

---

### FAQ 2

**Question:** How long does printing take?

**Answer:**
Turnaround depends on the size and complexity of your project. Most prints are completed within 5-10 business days from order to delivery. Rush services are available for tight deadlines.

---

### FAQ 3

**Question:** Can you fix my file if it won't print?

**Answer:**
Absolutely! File repair is one of our specialties. Upload your file for a free assessment, and we'll tell you exactly what needs fixing and how much it'll cost.

---

### FAQ 4

**Question:** Do I need a 3D file, or can you design one for me?

**Answer:**
Both! If you have a file, we'll print it. If you don't, our design team can create one from scratch based on your sketches, photos, or ideas.

---

### FAQ 5

**Question:** What materials do you recommend?

**Answer:**
It depends on your project! We'll recommend the best material based on whether you need strength, detail, flexibility, or a specific finish. Our team provides expert guidance with every order.

---

### FAQ 6

**Question:** Where do you ship?

**Answer:**
We ship anywhere in the UK with fast, reliable delivery. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

---

### FAQ 7

**Question:** What if I'm not happy with my print?

**Answer:**
We stand behind our work. If there's an issue with your print, contact us and we'll make it right‚Äîwhether that's a reprint or a full refund.

---

### FAQ 8

**Question:** Can I see examples of your work?

**Answer:**
Of course! Check out our [Projects Gallery] to see recent prints, or follow us on Instagram [@quirkable.uk] for behind-the-scenes content.
