#### FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling)

### File Best Practices

#### Wall Thickness
**Minimum recommended:** 1.5mm for FDM, 0.8mm for Resin

**Why it matters:** Thin walls may not print reliably or may be too fragile.

**We'll help:** If your walls are too thin, we'll recommend thickening them or switching materials.

---

#### File Size
**Maximum file size:** 500MB

**Recommended:** Under 100MB for faster processing

**Large files?** Contact us‚Äîwe can handle massive projects with special upload links.

---

#### Units & Scale
**Always specify:** mm, cm, or inches in your CAD software

**Double-check scale:** A 1mm miniature vs. a 100mm miniature is a big difference!

**We verify:** We'll confirm dimensions before printing.

---

#### Overhangs
**Angles over 45¬∞:** May need support material

**Support marks:** We minimize them, but some may be visible

**Design tip:** Orient overhangs strategically when designing.

---

## Section 9: FAQs

### Headline
**Your Questions Answered**

---

**Q: How do I know if my file will print successfully?**

A: Upload it! We provide a free file review within 24 hours. We'll tell you if there are any issues and how to fix them (or we can fix them for you).

---

**Q: What if I don't know which material to choose?**

A: That's what we're here for. Tell us what the print is for (decorative, functional, outdoor, etc.) and we'll recommend the best material and explain why.

---

**Q: Can you print in multiple colors?**

A: For single-piece multi-color, we can print using filament changes (manual color switching). For complex multi-color, we recommend printing parts separately or hand-painting.

---

**Q: How accurate are the prints?**

A: FDM: ¬±0.2mm typically. Resin: ¬±0.05mm. SLS: ¬±0.1mm. Actual accuracy depends on geometry, size, and material. We'll advise if your tolerances are critical.

---

**Q: Do you offer rush printing?**

A: Yes! Rush service can cut turnaround by 50% for an additional 30-50% cost. Contact us for availability.

---

**Q: Can you print really large objects?**

A: Our maximum print volume is 30cm √ó 30cm √ó 40cm. For larger objects, we can print in sections and provide assembly guidance.

---

**Q: What's the smallest detail you can print?**

A: Resin printing can achieve details as small as 0.1mm. FDM minimum detail is around 0.4mm. We'll advise if your details are too fine.

---

**Q: Can I get a test print first?**

A: For large projects, we can print a small test section or scaled-down version. Contact us to discuss.
