## Testing & Selection

### Criteria for Good Taglines

1. **Memorable:** Can you recall it after hearing once?
2. **Unique:** Does it differentiate from competitors?
3. **Clear:** Is the meaning immediately obvious?
4. **Relevant:** Does it reflect the brand promise?
5. **Timeless:** Will it work in 5+ years?
6. **Flexible:** Does it work across all services?
7. **Authentic:** Does it match how Quirkable actually operates?

---

**Main Tagline:** "Turn Ideas Into Objects."
- Use everywhere: website, signatures, footer
