## Week 1: Legal & Financial Foundation

### Day 1-2: Business Registration

**Actions:**
1. Register with Companies House as a limited company
   - Suggested name: "Quirkable Ltd"
   - Use provided brand documentation for business description
   - Director(s) and shareholding structure

   - Corporation Tax
   - PAYE (if hiring immediately)

- gov.uk/set-up-business

## Emergency Troubleshooting

### "Sales are slower than expected"
1. Review pricing (too high?)
2. Check marketing (reaching right people?)
3. Improve website (conversion issues?)
4. Increase advertising spend
5. Partner with influencers
6. Offer limited-time promotion

### "Can't keep up with orders"
1. Increase prices (manage demand)
2. Hire part-time help
3. Buy more equipment
4. Streamline processes
5. Set realistic lead times
6. Consider pausing Ready-Made to focus on higher-margin work

### "Equipment keeps breaking"
1. Regular maintenance schedule
2. Buy backup equipment
3. Maintenance fund (¬£200-500/month)
4. Extended warranties
5. Repair skills development

### "Cash flow problems"
1. Require deposits (50% upfront)
2. Faster payment terms
7. Reduce inventory
8. Cut non-essential costs
9. Consider invoice financing
10. Speak to accountant
