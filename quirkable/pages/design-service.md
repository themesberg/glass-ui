## Section 7: FAQs

### Headline
**Common Questions About Our Design Service**

---

**Q: I only have a rough sketch. Is that enough to start?**

A: Absolutely! Rough sketches are perfect starting points. We'll work with you to refine the concept and clarify details through the design process.

---

**Q: Can you design from just a description, no images?**

A: Yes, though having visual references helps tremendously. We'll discuss your vision in detail and provide concept sketches before committing to full 3D modeling.

---

**Q: How many revisions do I get?**

A: Depends on your package. Standard packages include 2-3 revision rounds. Additional rounds available for a fee. We want you happy, so we're flexible.

---

**Q: What if I need changes after the project is complete?**

A: Minor tweaks within 30 days are usually free. Larger modifications are quoted separately but discounted since we know the project.

---

**Q: Do I need to print with you, or can I take the file elsewhere?**

A: Your choice completely! You own the file. That said, we offer a discount if we print what we design (since we know it'll work perfectly).

---

**Q: Can you match an existing art style?**

A: Usually yes. Provide reference examples and we'll match the aesthetic as closely as possible.

---

**Q: How do you handle confidential or proprietary designs?**

A: We offer NDAs for sensitive projects. Your intellectual property is protected and never shared without written permission.

---

**Q: Can you design something to be sold commercially?**

A: Yes! You own the file and can use it commercially. We can discuss commercial-scale considerations and licensing if you're selling many copies.

---

**Q: What's the most complex thing you've designed?**

A: We've designed everything from intricate jewelry to functional mechanical assemblies with multiple moving parts. If it's physically possible to print, we can design it.

---

**Q: Do you offer 3D scanning services?**

A: Not in-house, but we can recommend partners and work with scan data to create clean, printable models.
