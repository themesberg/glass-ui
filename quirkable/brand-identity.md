## 2. What Makes Quirkable, Quirkable

**You don't just print.**

You sculpt, refine, fix, design, and bring ideas to life.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Specialize in prints that other shops would reject
- Offer a seamless path from sketch to object
- Handle the entire journey from idea to finished product
- Combine technical expertise with creative problem-solving

---

## 3. Brand Promise

Quirkable helps people turn wild ideas and wonky files into high-quality, finished 3D objects.

Whether someone needs a custom figurine, a prototype part, a bespoke gift, or a repaired file that's driving them mad, Quirkable handles the entire journey.

---

## 4. Core Services Overview

### A. Ready-Made 3D Models (Quirkable Originals)

Professionally designed models created in-house and sold via the Quirkable shop.

**What's Included:**
- High-quality resin and filament prints
- Ornaments, toys, décor, functional parts
- Exclusive creator-made designs
- Premium finishes (matte, gloss, painted options)

**Target Users:**
Collectors, gift buyers, home décor lovers, hobbyists

---

### B. Bespoke Printing Service

**You give the file. Quirkable prints it to perfection.**

**Process:**
1. Upload your file through a smooth online form
2. Automated or manual review for printability
3. Material options suggested
4. Transparent quotation
5. Printing, post-processing, and delivery

**What Quirkable Prints:**
- STL, OBJ, 3MF, STEP, and CAD exports
- Custom parts, prototypes, cosplay items, miniatures
- Product samples, replacement parts, models, and proofs

**Why It Stands Out:**
High repeatability, clean finishes, great communication, and solid turnaround times.

---

### C. 3D File Fixing & Repair

**The magical service that turns chaotic meshes into print-ready beauty.**

**Common Issues Fixed:**
- Holes in mesh
- Non-manifold geometry
- Thin walls
- Overhangs and impossible geometry
- File optimization for strength or detail
- Model hollowing
- Repairing broken or messy scans

**Ideal User:**
Anyone who has a 3D model but keeps hearing "file not printable".

---

### D. Full 3D Design Service

**No file? No problem. Quirkable builds it.**

**Options:**
- Design from scratch (sketch to CAD)
- Convert 2D drawings or images into 3D
- Product development (early-stage prototyping)
- Custom figurines or personal items
- Iteration and versioning
- One-to-one design consultation

**Tools & Expertise:**
Fusion 360, Blender, ZBrush, CAD packages depending on style.

**Outcome:**
A fully print-ready, fully owned digital model.

## 5. Technologies & Materials

### Technologies Offered:
- **FDM** (Fused Deposition Modeling)
- **SLA** (Resin printing)
- **SLS** (Selective Laser Sintering)
- **High-detail resin**
- Optional partner manufacturing for metal or nylon runs

### Materials:
PLA, ABS, PETG, resin, tough resin, flexible resin, nylon options, wood-blend filaments, and specialty composites.
